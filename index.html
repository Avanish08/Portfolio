About Me

I’m a multidisciplinary creative with a passion for visual storytelling and interactive experiences. My work spans across game design, 2D art, animation, and narrative development. Whether it's designing gameplay mechanics, crafting compelling storylines, or breathing life into characters through animation, I aim to create experiences that resonate emotionally and aesthetically.

With a background in graphic design and a strong foundation in visual composition, I approach every project with both artistic vision and technical skill. I enjoy collaborating with teams, iterating ideas, and turning imagination into immersive digital worlds.

I'm always exploring new tools, trends, and techniques to push the boundaries of creativity. Whether you're looking for a game designer, visual artist, or storyteller — I’m excited to bring your ideas to life.
